Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

This privacy policy describes how the soscraper Chrome extension handles your information.

Information Collection and Use

soscraper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. The extension operates locally in your browser and does not send any information to external servers.

Permissions

The permissions requested by the extension are only used to provide its core functionality. No data is collected or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.
